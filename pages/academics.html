Core Liberal Arts

AUIB's Core Liberal Arts (CLA) program is a series of courses required for all AUIB students. Our multi-disciplinary approach sets AUIB apart from other universities by providing students a breadth of knowledge and preparation in the humanities, social sciences, computer science and the natural sciences. The concept of “liberal arts” instruction dates back to the origins of higher education, aimed at preparing the student for a complete life as an intellectual. Giving students exposure to a variety of academic areas of interest such as history, philosophy and sociology as well as computer science and mathematics gives them the strong foundation that will allow them to confidently excel in their specialized major fields of inquiry.
